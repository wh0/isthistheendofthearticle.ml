Submit an encounter

Prepare two screenshots:

  • an "above" screenshot showing a transition from article text to another element, where it can't be seen what's farther below the other element; and
  • a "below" screenshot showing what's below the other element.

If the article does not end at the transition, then prefer to have the "below" screenshot show both the other element and the article text that follows.

Then, follow these steps to submit your encounter. You'll need a GitHub account for this.

  1. Upload "above" and "below" screenshots into the shots directory and open a pull request.

  2. Paste the pull request URL here and click 'Analyze' to fill in most of the fields in the compile form.

  3. If this continues from an existing encounter, select that encounter to fill in some more fields in the compile form.

  4. Compile form. Complete the fields with bolded labels manually, then click 'Compile' to transfer the information into the next form.
